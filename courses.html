Our Courses

Intensive Drift Course

Two days of the most efficient and professional drift course to become a real drifter! From the basic drills of initiating a drift car to aggressive transitions maneuvers and a hi-speed smoky drifting in a track layout, this course has it all. With a ratio of 2 drivers per drift car and lots of practice time, the participants learn to control the drift techniques, understand the sport of drifting and progress to a competitive drifting level. An official license is issued to each graduate by the end of the course, which allows them to compete in a European drift competitions with team academy's support.

Advanced Drift Course

Advanced Drift Course We've set up the perfect environment for our graduates and professional drifters; *4 professional drift competition layouts, for advanced drift practice. With an upgraded ratio of ONE driver per drift car, in a 2 days course, the participants will practice high speed drifting, mastering the use of the e-brake and progress through real drift competition tasks, such as; increasing the attack speed & angle, clicking the judged clipping points, choosing the fast lane and of course, increasing smoke volume... *All track layouts are different each Advanced Course

With a ratio of 3 drivers per drift car, the day is starting with a short briefing, to give the drivers the basic knowledge on how to drift a car.
